    Investing Responsibly for Sustainable Growth

    Sustainable growth and long-term value creation go hand-in-hand with responsible investing. Our commitment to Environmental, Social, and Governance (ESG) principles forms a core part of our investment strategy. As a Category 1 Angel Fund, we prioritise investing in startups that demonstrate solid financial potential and adhere to responsible business practices.

    Importance of ESG Aspects

    We understand the importance of ESG aspects and have integrated ESG risk management into our operations. Startfresh Ventures, being a responsible investor, is committed to ensuring adherence by all our portfolio companies to the ESG standards prescribed by IFC, CDC & DFC. We aim to bring positive impact by combining financial performance with the values of our stakeholders to benefit people and the planet in the long term.

    Do you have any Queries ?

    For any questions or concerns about the Fund’s Environmental, Social, & Governance Policy and/or the portfolio investments financed by the fund, please contact us at compliance@startfreshventures.com. We will respond within a reasonable time. Where the question or concern relates to a portfolio investment financed by the Fund, the interested or affected person(s) are first encouraged to discuss the matter directly with the portfolio company. In cases where the concerns are not addressed sufficiently at the portfolio company level, please email us.
