    About Our Firm

    Get to Know Our Experienced Startfresh Ventures

    We are a SEBI-registered Category 1-Angel Alternative Investment Fund (AIF) dedicated to unlocking India’s vast potential by investing in the future drivers of its economic growth. We provide early-stage and growth capital to innovative businesses across sectors. We believe that India's growth story is at a pivotal moment, and we are excited to play a vital role in fostering and scaling businesses poised to redefine industries.

    We are passionate about supporting entrepreneurs on their journey. Beyond investing capital, we help in business optimisation, mentorship, strategic insight, governance, talent sourcing and a network of partners; we aim to empower visionary founders to create sustainable, impactful, and scalable ventures. Our sector-agnostic approach allows us the flexibility to back high-potential companies across various Industries.

    At Startfresh Ventures, we hold a strong belief in India's economic potential. We envision India at the forefront of global innovation, and we are committed to playing a significant role in this journey.
